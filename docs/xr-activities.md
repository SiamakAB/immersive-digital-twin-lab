# XR Activities for DIWA Roadmap

> This document outlines recommended XR activities to support the long-term goals of the Immersive Digital Twin Lab within the DIWA Flagship.  
> It is intended as a draft for discussion and may be refined over time.

## Table 2 – Recommended XR Activities

| Activity | Type | Frequency | Expected Impact |
|----------|------|-----------|----------------|
| XR Technology Newsletter | Short-Term | Bi-weekly | Share updates, trends, and news around XR for the research community and DIWA stakeholders. Keeps the community informed and engaged. |
| One-Day XR Workshop | Short-Term | Biannual | Provide hands-on training on XR tools and applications for students, researchers, and staff, including practical sessions in the Immersive Digital Twin Lab. |
| Online XR Development Course | Medium-Term | Ongoing, subscription-based | Structured learning for VR/MR and digital twin applications accessible online for national and international participants. Builds capacity and broadens access to XR expertise. |
| Collaboration with Other XR Labs | Medium-Term | Ongoing | Establish partnerships with national and international XR labs to share expertise, resources, and best practices. Enhances research quality and global networking. |
| Participation in XR Events | Long-Term | Annually / As scheduled | Attend key XR conferences and events (e.g., XR4EUROPE 2026, Slush 2026, UnitedXR Europe, EGU Vienna 2026) to showcase work, learn new technologies, and network. Increases visibility and collaboration opportunities. |
| XR Demo Days / Showcases | Short-Term | Biannual | Present ongoing XR projects, prototypes, and research results to the academic and industry community using the Immersive Digital Twin Lab. Supports stakeholder engagement and feedback collection. |
| XR Research Publications | Medium-Term | Quarterly / As research is completed | Publish technical reports, case studies, and research findings on XR and digital twin applications. Enhances academic impact and project visibility. |
| XR Infrastructure Development | Long-Term | Ongoing | Build and maintain XR labs, VR/MR equipment, and software platforms—including the Immersive Digital Twin Lab—to support research, teaching, and demonstrations. Ensures sustainability and continuous technological advancement. |

---

> ⚠️ **Note:** This XR activities roadmap is a draft and intended for discussion. Feedback and recommendations are welcome to refine priorities, timing, and scope of activities.
